RESEARCH
New superconductivity theory
We have been constructing a new superconductivity theory that can explain cuprate superconductivity and the conventional BCS superconductors. This requires fundamental revisions of the current standard theory based on the BCS theory.
New quibts using superconductors and nanotechonology
We are building new quibts that can realize a fully-fault tolerant quantum computers.
Quantum theory of natural language
We investigate Natural Language as a strongly-correlated quantum system. This effort may elucidate what "Logos" is.
